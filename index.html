Bio

Hello! I'm a Master's student at School of Statistics, Renmin University of China under the guidance of Prof. Hongteng Xu and Prof. Hanfang Yang. I'm also a visiting research intern at Carnegie Mellon University, co-advised by Prof. Shixiang (Woody) Zhu and Prof. Holly Wiberg. My research interests lie in machine learning and statistical theories for sequential data modeling and sequential decision making. Specifically, I focus on Bayesian methods, point processes, and learning theory.
